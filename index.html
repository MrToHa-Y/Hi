FEAR OF GOD AND ESSENTIALS

Fear of God is an independent American luxury fashion label, founded in 2013 in Los Angeles by Jerry Lorenzo. Crafting timeless, wearable garments, the brand’s distinct interpretation of the American expression has become an emblem of contemporary culture. Paying homage to the unique heritage with a contemplated and sophisticated fusion through high-grade materials and fine craftsmanship.

Further details

Autograph of Jerry Lorenzo

The autograph of Jerry Lorenzo, founder of Fear of God, has come to symbolize the uniqueness and authenticity of the brand. Lorenzo, with no formal fashion training, created a signature style that reflects his personality and religious beliefs. The brand's name, linked to his spirituality, emphasizes the designer's personal approach to fashion and distinguishes it from mainstream brands.

Brand advancement

The Essentials line, which is more affordable, has allowed the brand to expand its customer base by 50-60%

Over the past decade, the brand has expanded its sales geography, increasing its international presence by about 70%, becoming popular not only in the U.S. but also in Europe, Asia and other regions.

Collaborations with Nike, Vans and Ermenegildo Zegna have significantly increased awareness and audience reach, possibly by 30-40%

FEAR OF GOD X ADIDAS COLLECTION

COLLECTION 8 “BEHIND THE VISION”

Jerry Lorenzo's eighth collection, Fear of God 2023, was one of the brand's most anticipated collections and symbolized its evolution from streetwear to luxury fashion with a more refined aesthetic. The collection combined American style with elements of classic wear and modern elegance, reflecting Lorenzo's unique take on style.
